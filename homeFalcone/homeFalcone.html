About Finding Falcone

The story is from the planet of Lengaburu…in the distant distant galaxy of Tara B. After the recent war with neighbouring planet Falicornia, King Shan has exiled the Queen of Falicornia for 15 years.
Queen Al Falcone is now in hiding. But if King Shan can ﬁnd her before the years are up, she will be exiled for another 15 years…
King Shan has received intelligence that Al Falcone is in hiding in one of these 6 planets - DonLon, Enchai, Jebing, Sapir, Lerbin & Pingasor. However he has limited resources at his disposal & can send his army to only 4 of these planets.

Finding Falcone Game - User Interface Help

This Lists the Details of Planets and Space Vehicles used in Finding Falcone Game.
Planets
  • There are 6 planets. But King Shan can send vehicles to search in only 4 at a time.
  • All are at varying distances from Lengaburu.
Space Vehicles
  • There are 4 types of vehicles
  • The units of each vehicle type vary (eg:- there are 2 space pods but only 1 space rocket)
  • All have different ranges (maximum distance it can travel).
  • All have different speeds.

Predict the Planets where Al Falcone may be hiding by selceting from the Dropbox as shown in the image.

Plan and Select the right Space Vehicle for each of the Planet, each time a selection is made the Total Time Taken at the Top Right Corner and the Time Taken for each individual selection is updated as shown in the image.

Once all the selections is done, the button Find Falcone...!!! as shown in the image gets enabled(Would be disabled until all the selections are made), Once this button is clicked it redirects to a new page which shows whether Al Falcone was found and its associated details. Also as seen in the image there is a Reset Finding Falcone button which can be used to reset all the selections made and start afresh.
